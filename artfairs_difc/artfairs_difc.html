DIFC Art Nights

PRESS RELEASE

DIFC Art Nights 1-2 April 2021

Coinciding the much-anticipated Dubai Art Week where DIFC is hosting Art Dubai, the world’s largest international art fair, Alexandra Gallery was honored to take part in the 11th edition of DIFC Art Nights on the 1st and 2nd of April 2021 in the Gate Village, Dubai.

Annually DIFC Art Nights exhibit an exceptional breadth of art installations, sculptures and paintings that are looking towards the future and aim at creating positive experiences for the residents and visitors.

Visitors are transported into a world of creativity and inspiration as they enjoy eclectic art pieces from galleries such as Christie’s, Opera Gallery, Sconci Gallery, Sotheby’s, Tabari Art Space and now, of course, Alexandra Gallery.

This year Alexandra Gallery presented talented artists from Russia and Spain:

  • Alexander YaYa
  • Will YaYa
  • Mercedes Castro Lomas
  • Daria Kolosova
  • Maria Kova
