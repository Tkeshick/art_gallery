ABOUT GALLERY

Alexandra gallery is a fine art gallery dedicated to the promotion of Russian, European and USA artists seeking exposure to the Dubai art market. Alexandra gallery specialises in modern and contemporary art working closely with emerging and established international artists. From its inception, Alexandra Gallery has championed painters and sculpture who display exceptional talent, technical skill and intrinsic creativity and with whom we have worked to produce world-class art exhibition that translate to a global audience. Alexandra Gallery connects artists with professionals, art collectors, and other artists to create an ever growing family dedicated to the world of fine art.
